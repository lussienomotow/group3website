Philippine Hot Air Balloon Festival

Where

Clark Freeport, Pampanga

When

Celebrated between January and February

Description

The Philippine Hot Air Balloon Festival is an annual event held in Clark Freeport Zone, Pampanga, Philippines. It's a thrilling spectacle featuring colorful hot air balloons of various shapes and sizes, taking flight against the backdrop of the sky. The festival typically includes hot air balloon flights, aerobatic exhibitions, skydiving performances, kite-flying displays, and other aviation-related activities.














"The weekend of everything that flies"

HIGHLIGHTS

The most exciting part of a hot air balloon festival is arguably the mass ascension, where numerous balloons take to the sky simultaneously, creating a vibrant and awe-inspiring spectacle. The sight of colorful balloons filling the air against the backdrop of the horizon is truly exhilarating and memorable.
