Panagbenga Festival

Where

Originated at Baguio City

When

held every 1st of February

Description

The Panagbenga Festival is a famous event held in Baguio City, Philippines, typically during the month of February. It's also known as the Baguio Flower Festival. "Panagbenga" means "season of blooming" in the local Kankanaey language. The festival celebrates the city's colorful flowers and the rich cultural heritage of the Cordilleran people.














"Season of Blooming"

HIGHLIGHTS

The highlight of the festival is the grand street parade, where participants showcase elaborate floats adorned with flowers, depicting various themes and designs. There are also street dancing competitions featuring dancers in vibrant costumes performing traditional and contemporary dances.Throughout the festival, visitors can enjoy a variety of activities such as flower exhibits, landscaping competitions, art fairs, and food stalls offering local delicacies. The Panagbenga Festival is not only a celebration of flowers but also a reflection of the unity, creativity, and resilience of the people of Baguio City and the Cordillera region.
