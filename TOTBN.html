Traslacion of the Black Nazarene Festival

Where

Originated at Manila (Quiapo Church)

When

Celebrated every January 9th

Description

The Traslacion of the Black Nazarene is an annual religious procession in Manila, Philippines, . It commemorates the transfer of the image of the Black Nazarene from Intramuros to the Minor Basilica of the Black Nazarene (Quiapo Church). The procession draws millions of devotees who participate in the procession, often barefoot, to show their devotion and seek blessings. The event is marked by fervent prayers, chanting, and a sense of spiritual reverence as devotees seek to touch or get near the revered image of the Black Nazarene for blessings and miracles. The Traslacion is one of the largest religious events in the Philippines and reflects the deep faith and devotion of the Filipino people.
